Our Story

Passion in Every Pour

Founded in 2015, Aurum began with a simple mission: to serve coffee that inspires. Nestled in the heart of the city, we source ethically grown beans from the world's finest regions and roast them to perfection.

We believe coffee is more than just a drink; it's a ritual, a moment of pause, and a connector of people. Our cozy space is designed to be your home away from home.
